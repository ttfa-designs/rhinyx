COMPANY PROFILE

Power that moves mines

Rhinyx is a South African-based mining and industrial support company specialising in heavy equipment hire, operational support services, and integrated mining solutions. We serve mining houses, contractors, and industrial clients operating in complex, high-demand environments where reliability, safety, and performance are critical.

Our business is built on a deep understanding of mining operations, regulatory compliance, and site-specific challenges. By combining dependable equipment, skilled personnel, and a solutions-driven approach, Rhinyx enables clients to maintain productivity, reduce downtime, and achieve sustainable operational performance.

MISSION

Transforming Mining Operations

At Rhinyx, our mission is to transform the mining industry by delivering innovative, high-quality solutions and dependable heavy equipment that enable mining and industrial operations to perform at their best in complex and demanding environments.

We partner with our clients to address critical operational challenges by providing practical, integrated, and forward-thinking solutions that enhance efficiency, improve reliability, and support safe, continuous production. Through strict compliance with health, safety, and environmental standards, we aim to reduce downtime, optimise asset performance, and support responsible mining practices.

VISION

Leading the Industry Forward

Our vision is to create a lasting and transformative impact in South Africa and beyond by strengthening the mining industry, elevating industrial standards, and driving continuous innovation in a safe, responsible, and sustainable manner.

We aspire to be the partner of choice for mines and industrial enterprises by consistently delivering excellence in quality, safety performance, environmental stewardship, and service delivery. Through technical competence, reliability, and strong governance, Rhinyx seeks to set new benchmarks for high-performing mining support solutions.
